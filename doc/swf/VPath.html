Classpublic class VPath
InheritanceVPath Object

VPath contains a list of continuous edges. There are two kinds of VPath: 1. Right fill path, 2. Left fill path. Left fill path is seen as normalized to right fill path in this class.

Right fill edges will be drawn in order, and left fill edges will be drawn in reversed order.
